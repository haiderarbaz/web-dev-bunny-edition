## Quick Facts

- **Created**: 1995 by Brendan Eich (in just 10 days!)
- **Original Name**: Mocha, then LiveScript, finally JavaScript
- **Game Changer**: V8 engine (2008) - written in C++, uses JIT compilation
- **Breakthrough**: Node.js (2009) - JavaScript outside the browser
- **Current State**: Full-stack language powering billions of devices
